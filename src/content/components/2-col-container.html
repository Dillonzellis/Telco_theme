Switch Kit

Our Switch Kit contains forms that will help you contact the companies and financial institutions that handle your automatic deposits and withdrawals. Anyone in Member Services will be happy to assist you with filling out the forms. We even include a convenient checklist to help you keep track of people you may need to contact.
