Direct Deposit

  • You can arrange to have a portion or your whole payroll, Social Security, pension, Tax Refund, or any other recurring check deposited directly into your Telco Plus Credit Union checking account or savings account. This eliminates the possibility of misplacing your paycheck and you won’t need to make a trip to cash or deposit it.
  • Receive your funds early! Payroll direct deposit is paid 2 days early if the file is sent in early.
  • Talk to your HR department to set up direct or talk to Member Services for our Direct Deposit form.
Why should you use Direct Deposit?
  • Safe – Your paycheck is protected against loss or theft.
  • Worry-free – You can be assured that your check is deposited, even when you’re away from home.
  • Saves time – You no longer have to make special trips to the credit union or stand in line on payday.
  • Convenient – No more waiting for the mail. Your deposits are automatically made for you, allowing you to set your own timetable for accessing your funds.

Are you having trouble making a mobile deposit?

Here are some tips to try:

  • Make sure there are no creases or folds in the check.
  • Check your photo quality:
    • Use a solid background when taking a photo; try solid white or black for contrast.
    • Make sure all 4 corners of the check are well within the photo.
    • Make sure there is no glare or too much light on the check; the information on the check needs to be readable.
  • If you have recently changed your password, you might need to wait for the system to sync up.
  • Try uninstalling the app and reinstalling it.
  • If you are still having trouble, please call Member Services.
