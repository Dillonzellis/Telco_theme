How to check your balance.
  • Log into your mobile app or Home Banking.
  • You can call audio response, 903-757-6014.
  • Enter your account number #
  • Enter your PIN code #
  • Enter Your Transaction Code # (30 is Checking, 31 is Savings, 32 is Loan Balance).
How to change password.
  • Log into Home Banking
  • Click on the person icon in the top right corner.
  • Click on Profile & Settings
  • Click on Change Password
  • Fill in the information.
How to change your username.
  • Log into Home Banking
  • Click on the person icon in the top right corner.
  • Click on Profile & Settings
  • Click on Change Username
  • Fill in the information.
  • The change is immediate.
