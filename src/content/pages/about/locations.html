Shared Branches Locations
If you are visiting Telco Plus as a guest member, please read what services and documents are required.
Transactions You Can Typically Do...
  • Deposit and Withdraw Funds ($500 maximum here at Telco Plus)
  • Account Inquiries and Statement Printing
  • Transfers
  • Loan Payments
  • Loan Advances
  • Purchase Money Orders and Official Checks, max $2,999.00 (prices vary at each institution)
What you will need...
  • Valid State-Issued Driver’s License or Identification Card or Resident Card or Passport
  • Your member number!
  • Our full name – Telco Plus Credit Union.
  • The last four numbers of your Social Security Number.
Important to know...
  • Each Credit Union will supply a Transaction Slip to fill out.
  • Shared Branches cannot see your passwords.
  • No third party checks will be accepted.
  • Since services vary from location to location, check with individual branches before you visit for a complete menu of available services.
  • Funds availability policies are set by each home credit union. Telco Plus members, please see our Funds Availability Policy.
  • Drive Thru and ATM Deposits are not always available to “Guest Members”.
  • Telco Plus CU does transactions in the lobby only for Shared Guest.
