We started out as East Texas Telco Credit Union on June 26, 1951, as a state-chartered credit union in a garage of a Longview home. The credit union served employees of Southwestern Bell Telephone Company in Longview. As the credit union grew through mergers and members referring their friends and family to the credit union, the location was moved to its current location. A second branch in Tyler was added in 1996. In 1997, the board chose to change the name of the credit union to Telco Plus Credit Union to better reflect the expanded membership base.

Membership

Please join Telco Plus Credit Union today! You can join, if you live or work in Gregg or Smith County, or through a family member, or work for any of our 100+ sponsor companies. If you are not sure if you are eligible for membership, please give us a call for help. Membership will give you access to low loan rates, lower fees, and lots of free services like bill pay and our mobile app.

When you open an account, we will ask for your name, address, date of birth, and other information that will allow us to identify you. We will also ask to see an identifying document and we do accept ITIN and Mexican Passports. Federal law, The USA Patriot Act, requires all financial institutions to obtain, verify and record information that identifies each person who opens an account. Come see us to open your account, it only takes about 20 minutes. You can use the checklist and forms from our Switch Kit to make the process even faster and easier.

Community Involvement

Since Telco Plus Credit Union is a community credit union, we actually support our community. Our wonderful, dedicated staff volunteers time and raises money for several great organizations in our area. We raise money every November for Longview PD and Tyler PD’s Project Blue Santa, collect peanut butter for the East Texas Food Bank’s backpack program; pick up trash for Keep Longview Beautiful and Keep Tyler Beautiful; we are a drop off location for Cellphones for Soldiers; and we offer free Financial Education to local schools and a $500 scholarship. We even help our Sponsor Companies when they need extra hands to run events.

If you too would like to support our efforts, please see our staff, or help these great organizations directly! Be sure to follow our social media pages for current efforts.
